FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Who are your lessons suitable for?

+

Lessons are designed for school students, university students, working professionals, and anyone preparing for Cambridge, IELTS, SAT or Professional English.

Do you offer an assessment session?

+

Yes. Every new student begins with an initial assessment session. During this session, we'll discuss your goals, evaluate your current English level, identify strengths and areas for improvement, and recommend the most suitable learning plan. A small assessment fee applies.

Are lessons conducted online or in person?

+

Lessons are currently conducted online, allowing flexible scheduling while maintaining an interactive and personalised learning experience.

How are lessons personalised?

+

Every student receives a customised learning plan based on their current level, objectives, strengths, and areas that require improvement. Lessons are continuously adjusted as progress is made.

How can international students make payments?

+

Students outside Vietnam can make payments through Wise, allowing secure transfers in their local currency. Vietnamese students may pay by local bank transfer. Full payment details are provided with each monthly invoice, and I'm happy to assist if you have any questions about the payment process.

When are tuition fees due?

+

Monthly invoices are issued on the 25th of each month for the following month's lessons.

Can lessons be rescheduled?

+

Yes. If you need to reschedule a lesson, please provide at least 24 hours' notice whenever possible.
