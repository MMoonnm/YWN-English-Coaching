STUDENT AND PARENT POLICIES

Clear Policies.
Better Learning.

These policies are designed to create a professional, respectful and productive learning environment for every student and family.

Last Updated: June 2026

By enrolling in lessons with English coach Yeo, students and parents acknowledge that they have read and agreed to the following policies. These guidelines help ensure lessons remain organised, effective and fair for everyone.

SECTION 01

Enrollment & Payment

Monthly Enrollment & Payment

Tuition is calculated based on the number of scheduled lessons for the upcoming month. An invoice will be issued on the 25th of each month. Payment is required before the first scheduled lesson of the new month. If payment has not been received before the second scheduled lesson, all remaining lessons will be suspended until payment has been completed.

Monthly Continuation Confirmation

Together with the monthly invoice, students or parents will be asked to confirm whether lessons will continue for the following month. This allows lesson schedules to be arranged in advance and ensures reserved lesson times remain available.

Notice of Withdrawal

Students wishing to discontinue lessons are kindly requested to notify me before the 25th of the current month. As lesson schedules are planned and reserved on a monthly basis, advance notice allows sufficient time to reorganise the teaching schedule and offer the reserved monthly lesson plan to another student. If no notice is received before the monthly confirmation date, lessons will be assumed to discontinue into the following month.

SECTION 02

Scheduling Policies

Reserved Lesson Time

Each student's weekly lesson time is reserved exclusively for them. Consistent scheduling supports long-term learning progress and ensures fairness for all students. Reserved lesson times may be offered to students on the waiting list if lessons are discontinued.

Lesson Duration

Lesson lengths are designed according to each programme. Cambridge Young Learners, School English and Communication English lessons are 60 minutes. IELTS and SAT Reading & Writing lessons are 90 minutes.

Rescheduling Policy

Students who need to reschedule should provide at least 24 hours' notice. Lessons cancelled with sufficient notice will be rescheduled based on mutual availability. Lessons cancelled less than one hour before the scheduled lesson may be considered completed unless the absence is due to an emergency. If I need to reschedule a lesson, a make-up lesson will always be arranged.

Late Arrival Policy

Lessons finish at their originally scheduled time. Students arriving late will still finish at the scheduled ending time, ensuring fairness for students with lessons afterwards.

Holiday & Long Break Policy

If a student plans to take a long break (such as holidays, examinations or summer programmes), please provide at least two weeks' notice. Depending on availability, lessons may be temporarily paused, rescheduled or the reserved lesson time released.

SECTION 03

Learning Expectations

Homework & Independent Study

Progress depends not only on lesson time but also on consistent independent practice. Students are expected to complete assigned homework, review lesson notes, revise vocabulary regularly, and practise speaking and writing between lessons. Homework should normally be completed before the next lesson or within one week unless otherwise instructed.

Progress Tracking

Every student follows an individual learning roadmap. Progress is continuously monitored across grammar, vocabulary, reading, writing, speaking, pronunciation and recurring mistakes. Lessons are adapted according to each student's progress rather than following a fixed textbook alone.

Assessment Session

Students may request a paid 30-minute diagnostic assessment before beginning long-term coaching. The assessment identifies current ability, strengths, weaknesses, learning goals and provides a recommended study roadmap.

SECTION 04

Communication

Communication Hours

To maintain work-life balance and ensure adequate lesson preparation, messages are normally answered between 10:00 and 21:00 (Vietnam Time). Messages received outside these hours will be answered as soon as reasonably possible.

Questions Outside Lessons

Students are encouraged to ask brief academic questions whenever clarification is needed. However, detailed explanations, essay corrections or solving multiple practice questions form part of scheduled coaching sessions and will be addressed during lessons.

SECTION 05

Professional Standards

Professional Learning Environment

Students should prepare all required materials, join lessons punctually and minimise distractions. Mutual respect and professional communication are expected from both coach and student throughout the learning journey.

Coach Responsibilities

The coach is committed to preparing lessons appropriate to each student's level, providing constructive feedback, monitoring progress continuously and maintaining a respectful, supportive and professional learning environment.

SECTION 06

General Policies

Recording Policy

Lessons may not be audio recorded, video recorded, distributed or shared without the prior consent of both the coach and the student.

Academic Integrity

Coaching is designed to develop genuine English proficiency. Assistance intended to facilitate plagiarism, cheating or academic dishonesty will not be provided.

Policy Updates

These policies may be updated periodically to reflect improvements to teaching practices or administrative procedures. The latest version published on this website supersedes all previous versions.
